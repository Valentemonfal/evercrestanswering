Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our service

What is an answering service?
An answering service is a business that answers phone calls on behalf of your company. Evercrest provides live, professional receptionists who answer your calls 24/7, take messages, schedule appointments, and capture lead information—so you never miss an important call.
How do you answer calls for my business?
You simply forward your business phone number to ours, or we can provide you with a dedicated number. When calls come in, our trained receptionists answer with your custom greeting, gather the information you need, and deliver it to you instantly via email, text, or your preferred method.
Do you provide bilingual receptionists?
Yes! We offer English and Spanish answering support. Our bilingual agents ensure you never lose a call from Spanish-speaking customers.
Can you schedule appointments?
Absolutely. Our receptionists can schedule appointments directly into your calendar. We integrate with popular scheduling tools and can work with your existing systems.
Do you work after business hours?
Yes! Evercrest operates 24 hours a day, 7 days a week—including weekends and holidays. Your business is always covered, no matter when calls come in.
